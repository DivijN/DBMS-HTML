Health Benefits of Donating Blood

Blood donation not only makes the receiverï¿½s life good but also helps the donor to maintain good health.It has the following benifits.

  1. Reduced risk of hemochromatosis

    • Health benefits of blood donation include reduced risk of hemochromatosis. Hemochromatosis is a health condition that arises due to excess absorption of iron by the body.

  2. Anti-cancer Benefits

    • By donating blood the iron stores in the body are maintained at healthy levels. A reduction in the iron level in the body is linked to low cancer risk.

  3. Maintains Healthy Heart & Liver

    • Blood donation is beneficial in reducing the risk of ailments caused by the iron overload in the body such as the risk of cirrhosis, liver failure, damage to the pancreas, and heart abnormalities.
