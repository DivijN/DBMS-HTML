The purpose of 'BLOOD BANK MANAGEMENT SYSTEM' is to effeciently use the donated blood and help the needy.
It also ensures hazel free blood donation process by organizing various donation camps.
The collected blood is further stored in appropriate conditions.
Through this web-based application donars can register and proceed with the donation process .
A general consumer can have a detailed view about the date of donation and the available blood groups in the blood bank.
Consumers can order for any available blood-groups which will be processed and acknowledged effeciently.
We ensure secure storage of personal details of the donars and the recipients which can be accessed only by authenticated users.
Thus,we proceed one step ahead in saving lives,by lending extended support during emergency.
